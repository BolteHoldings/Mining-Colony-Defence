Mining Colony Defense

Rival corporations are raiding your extraction network. Hold the line.
  • Enemies march the path toward your HQ. Any that reach it damage the HQ and the nearest mine. Lose the HQ and it's over — there are no "lives".
  • Each map has fixed ore nodes: Iron = +damage, Crystal = +range, Coal = +fire rate.
  • Build a Mine on a node, build Towers on open ground, then use Connect to wire a mine into a tower. Each mine powers one tower.
  • Mix resource types on one tower for synergies: Iron+Crystal = Precision (crits), Iron+Coal = Explosive (splash), Crystal+Coal = Targeting, all three = Overclocked.
  • Damaged mines produce less and give weaker bonuses — Repair them. Saboteurs hunt mines; EMP units cut your lines. Bosses hit every 10 waves.
